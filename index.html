About Lilac Valley Farm Stay

Lilac Valley Farm Stay is a rustic farm stay accommodation in the hills of the Blue Mountains, just 90 minutes from Sydney. The property is owned by Marina Ye, a local interior designer who purchased the property three years ago with a plan to renovate. After years of hard work, the property now features a beautifully restored, open plan, luxury barn house, on four acres of land home to sheep, chickens, ducks and one very friendly goat named Rosie. There is an outdoor plunge pool just off the back verandah, and a stunning cottage garden.

Lilac Valley Farm Stay is set in the World Heritage-listed Blue Mountains, a stay here transports you to picturesque countryside where you can experience the joys of a quintessential country lifestyle. Enjoy canoeing, bushwalks, swimming, catching yabbies, running around with your dog (yes, we're pet friendly!) ...or just kick back and enjoy the tranquil surrounds.

Your whole family will love the farm stay experience at Lilac Valley Farm Stay.
